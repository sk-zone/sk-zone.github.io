About Me

I am a graduate researcher in the Data Science & Machine Learning Lab under Muhammad U. S. Khan at NUST. My research focuses on developing large-scale learning systems suitable for real-world deployment. I am particularly interested in adaptive multimodal LLMs, human-in-the-loop methodologies, and privacy-preserving machine learning. My goal is to build machine learning frameworks that are accurate, scalable, equitable, robust, and aligned with societal well-being.
