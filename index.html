About Me

I am a graduate researcher in the Data Science & Machine Learning Lab under Muhammad U. S. Khan at NUST, working at the intersection of trustworthy ML and multimodal AI. My research focuses on developing fair, robust, and interpretable large-scale learning systems suitable for real-world deployment.

I am particularly interested in adaptive multimodal LLMs, human-in-the-loop methodologies and privacy-preserving machine learning.

Overall, my goal is to build machine learning frameworks that are accurate, scalable, equitable, transparent, and aligned with societal well-being.
